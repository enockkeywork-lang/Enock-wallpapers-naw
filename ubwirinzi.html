1. Ikinyabupfura n'Ikaridataba (Data Encryption & Storage)

Urubuga rwa ENOCK WALLPAPERS rukoresha uburyo buhambaye bwa End-to-End Encryption buvuga ko amakuru yose winjiza cyangwa ucyura asukurwa mu buryo bwa mudasidwa bwihariye. Imeri yawe ya Gmail cyangwa izindi kode z'ubwishyu ntizishobora kubonwa n'undi muntu uwo ari we wose cyangwa ngo zerekezwe ku zindi mbuga z'inyamahanga.

Amakuru yawe yose abikwa muri Secure Local Environment y'urubuga muri telefone yawe (Local Storage). Ibi bituma niyo waba udakafiye interineti, sisitemu ikomeza kwibuka igenamiterere ryawe nta kajagari.

💳

2. Umutekano w'Ubwishyu bwa Lumicash Burundi

Mu rwego rwo kurwanya uburiganya n'ubujura bwo kuri interineti mu Burundi, inzira zose z'ubwishyu zikoresha **Lumicash** (izina ry'uwanditswe: INGABIRE VIOLETTE kuri numero 64583963) zagenzuwe neza.

Iyo winjije kode ya SMS (TxID), sisitemu yacu iyitandukanya n'izindi mu buryo bwa automatic algorithm bupima niba iyo kode ikiri nshya kandi ihuye n'igiciro cy'ifoto (2,000 FBU cyangwa 2,500 FBU). Urubuga ntabwo rufata nimero yawe y'ibanga (PIN), bityo konti yawe ya Lumicash iguma ifite umutekano usesuye 100%.
